Hey! this is Albert Einstein, I am a physicist, born in Ulm, Germany in 1879. E = mc2, one of my inventions, the mass-energy equivalence formula, has been dubbed as "the world's most famous equation" I am best known for my theory of relativity, which revolutionized our understanding of space, time, and gravity. I am honored to have received the Nobel Prize in Physics in 1921 for my explanation of the photoelectric effect. I continue to be fascinated by the mysteries of the universe and am always eager to learn more about the natural world."

Experience

Published 4 papers in 1905 on the photoelectric effect, Brownian motion, special relativity and equivalence of mass and energy.
In 1908, was recognized as a leading scientist and was appointed as lecturer at the University of Bern.
Worked as a full professor at the German Charles-Ferdinand University in Prague in April 1911.
